What We Believe

The operating principles behind every engagement.

  • Payments adoption is a product problem, not a sales problem.
  • Most platforms choose their monetization model too early and change it too late.
  • The gap between Model 1 and Model 3 is usually 3× revenue. The implementation gap is rarely as large as people think.
  • A payments program that 60% of merchants use is worth more than one that 20% use at twice the take rate.
  • The best time to model your payments opportunity is before you've committed to a vendor. The second-best time is right now.
Experience

Built from the inside out.

Margin Labs was built on experience that runs the full stack — from banking infrastructure and BaaS platform product management to embedded payments strategy and VP-level product leadership at PE-backed vertical SaaS companies.

That means understanding the problem from every angle: the operator who doesn't know where to start, the processor who has a stack to sell, the board that wants to see the revenue model, and the engineering team that has to build it.

  • Embedded payments strategy across ISV Referral, Enhanced Residuals, PayFac-as-a-Service, and Full PayFac models — including live implementations and migrations.
  • Merchant onboarding and activation design — the part most platforms get wrong. Adoption strategy, KYC flow design, and the UX decisions that determine whether 20% or 80% of merchants actually use the program.
  • Processor and vendor negotiations — not just model selection, but term sheet review, pricing benchmarks, and creating competitive tension during active evaluations.
  • PE-backed SaaS environments — growth-stage operators, acquisition integration, and board-level payments strategy presentations. This is where most of the buying decisions actually get made.
  • Banking-as-a-Service and banking infrastructure product management — including deposit products, card programs, and payment rails for underserved and underbanked market segments.
