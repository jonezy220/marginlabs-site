The Margin Multiplier™
Four models. One right answer for your stage.

Most SaaS platforms sit on one of four embedded payments models — and most are on the wrong one. The Margin Multiplier maps your volume against all four to show you the revenue delta.

Model 01
ISV Referral
Lowest effort, lowest take rate. Refer merchants to a processor, earn a residual split. Good starting point.
~15–25 bps
Model 02
Enhanced Residuals
Negotiate deeper economics with your ISO or processor. Same infrastructure, better split.
~30–50 bps
Model 03
PayFac-as-a-Service
Own the merchant experience without full PayFac liability. Best economics for $5M–$50M ARR.
~60–100 bps
Model 04
Full PayFac
Maximum control and margin. Significant compliance overhead. Right for $50M+ ARR platforms.
~100–150 bps
